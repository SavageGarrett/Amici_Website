About

In order to study the formation and impact of interest group networks, the Amicus Curiae Networks Project created a database of all the interest groups (organizations, associations and corporations) that have signed onto amicus curiae briefs in the last 90 years. This list of cosignatories makes it possible for the first time to trace the purposive and coordinated behavior of a comprehensive collection of interest groups.
